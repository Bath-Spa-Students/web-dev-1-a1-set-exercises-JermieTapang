Japanese Beef Curry ビーフカレー

This flavorful and filling Japanese Beef Curry, with its soft beef, potatoes, carrots, mushrooms, and curry roux,
is a great way to introduce novice curry rice eaters to the dish.Tasted to suit Japanese palates,
it has a stew-like consistency and is sweet and mild. Even kids have a great time with it! This is such a simple dish that you must try.

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Onions, Potatoes, Carrots
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Wine
  • Beef Stock
  • Seasonings: tomato paste, Japanese curry powder, Japanese curry roux, bay leaf, etc.
  • Optional add-ins: grated apple, milk, Worcestershire sauce.

Overview: Cooking Steps:

  1. Cut the onions and start sautéing them until tender and caramelized.
  2. Meanwhile, cut all the vegetables and beef.
  3. Sear the beef and add it to the caramelized onion.
  4. Add the wine, stock, carrots, mushrooms, and seasonings, and cook for 2 hours (add the potatoes 15 minutes prior to finishing cooking in order to save their shape).
  5. Add Japanese curry roux and serve with rice!
